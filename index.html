What are lenses?

Lenses are used to be able to look at faraway objects much closer. Theres also different types of lenses such as platic, glass and even a combination of both. It is highly recommended to get glasss lenses because they offer much more clarity.

Often digital cameras tend to come with the body only and not the lenses. Most of the time you will have to just buy the lenses sepeartly which is often better because you get to choose the type of lense that you want wether it's small or big.
