In photography you CAN just take pctures with the regular settings you have on your phone, computer, or camera. However, there are other options to make your picture look way better. The phone camera quality isn't the same as a camera quality. But there are ways to make it look somewhat alike.

Snapseed mobile app is an app for mobile users who want to make their pictures look better or more creative. It is used for editing; changing exposure, contrast, saturation and more.

Adobe lightroom interface and DXO photlab 4 are both good editing apps that are used on laptop/computer but Adobe lightroom interface can also be used on phone. Adobe lightroom interface is not only a good app but it can be eaisly accesible. It has a left panel where you can toggle betwen pictures as you edit and a right panel where you can make quick adjustmets. DXO photolab is another good editing app it is known for being able to make a picture more smoother and clearer.
